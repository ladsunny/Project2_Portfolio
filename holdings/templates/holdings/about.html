About me

Hi, my name is Nishant Sharma. Investing in stocks is one of my favorite activities. Although I have been investing in US stock market since 2003, I really became passionate about it in 2010. Primarily, I like to invest in stocks of large US based companies that are publicly traded. And, I usually take a long-term view (1-5 years) when investing in these companies.


I am by no means an expert when it comes to stocks and investing. However, I strongly believe that I don't need to be a wizard to be successful with stocks. Instead, I need to be passionate, careful, patient, & persistent.


Currently, I manage my stock portfolio using basic tools such as Microsoft Excel, publicly available financial & market data, and a brokerage account. However, my current portfolio includes companies from multiple industries and sectors, and I am usually tracking performance of more than 100 companies on a regular basis.


Sidewithstocks.com is just another way for me to extend my passision with stocks. It compels me to be more thoughful about my investment decisions. It also provides me an alternate & customized source of reference. And, I expect that the information consolidated on this site will help me become a better investor.
