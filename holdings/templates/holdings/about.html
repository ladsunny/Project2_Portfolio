About me


Welcome to my blog site - sidewithstocks.com!

My name is Nishant Sharma. I am a retail investor, trading in US markets since 2003. My objective is to build & manage a strong equity portfolio, and try to achieve a better rate of return than S&P 500 index. I am not a professional investment advisor, and I also don't consider myself as an expert in managing money. However, I enjoy the process of researching publicly traded companies, and I take a lot of pride in managing my own portfolio.

Sidewithstocks.com is just another way for me to extend my passion for investing in stocks. It compels me to be more thoughtful about my investment decisions, and provides me a customized source of reference to track & analyze my recent trades. You are welcome to view all of this information, but please do not consider it as financial advice.
