About me

Hi, I am Nishant Sharma, and I am a retail investor. I have been investing in US stock market since 2003, however, I really became passionate about it in 2010. Typically, I invest in stocks of large US based companies that are publicly traded. And, I usually take a long-term view (1-5 years) when investing in these companies.


I am not a professional investment advisor, and I do not consider myself as an expert either. However, I take a lot of pride in managing my own stocks portfolio. It is a great feeling to be in full control of my investments, and to perform better than the benchmark.


Based on my experience in trading stocks for more than 15 years, I have come to believe that an investor does not need to be a wizard to be successful in the stock market, especially when investing in great companies for long-term. So, the idea is to keep it simple, and to not become impatient or greedy.


Sidewithstocks.com is just another way for me to extend my passion for investing in stocks. It compels me to be more thoughtful about my investment decisions, and provides me a customized source of reference to better manage my stocks portfolio.
